Fun Facts Friday ~

yes! You do need to wear sunblock in the winter. The need to protect your skin from harmful UV Rays is a year round commitment. UV exposure can cause fine lines and aging and intensify freckles and hyperpigmentation. your skin is affected by the UV rays that you encounter in your daily life, like driving.
